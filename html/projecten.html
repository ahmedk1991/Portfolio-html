Portfolio Website Creation

This project focused on developing a personal portfolio website using HTML and CSS.
The goal was to create a clean, beautiful interface to showcase my skills and projects
effectively. Emphasizing simplicity and visual appeal,the site was designed to highlight
my professional journey and technical devolopment in an engaging and accessible layout.

Project Evolution and Approach

The project kicked off with thorough planning, emphasizing a clear and beautiful design.
A minimalistic approach was adopted to enhance clarity and ease of navigation.
Careful attention was given to the visual elements, with a focus on typography and color choices,
aiming to strike a balance between aesthetic appeal and accessibility.
